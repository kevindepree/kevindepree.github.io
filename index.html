Bio

DePree began his musical career, like many drummers, playing in bars and cover bands but always had an interest in composing his own music. He eventually was introduced to Emmy Winning/BAFTA Nominated composer Sheridan Tongue who guided him through some of his first projects as a drum programmer & composer.

Over the span of 5+ years working for Tongue on his score productions, DePree has been able to develop his own approach as a live drummer/percussionist and drum programmer. Meerkat Manor: Rise of the Dynasty (BBC America, AMC+), Spotlight on The Troubles: A Secret History (BBC One Northern Ireland), Narco Wars (National Geographic), have all allowed DePree to refine his ability to deliver high-quality drums and percussion for film and television. DePree says, "I have a growing collection of percussion instruments, that by playing live, helps the music have a humanistic feel. Those live parts combined with programming my own custom sampled drums create a really classy modern sounding palette of drums and percussion".

Since moving to Los Angeles in 2020, DePree has been working at Drum Doctors under The Drum Doctor, Ross Garfield. DePree says, "Working here is like a drummer's dream, hands-on experience with the top studios, artists, and producers in LA. It has completely changed my understanding of drum tuning, teching, and playing in the recording studio environment. Brushing shoulders with the top session drummers working in LA isn't a bad perk either. It has been very cool meeting people in the music scene out here".

DePree also says, "I love composing and producing music! What really excites me is finding a new sound or concept to add to a track to make it unique to my style. Working in production music has been such a great way for me to develop my own sound. My goal from the start has always been, imitate great composers and producers so I can one day innovate. I am learning to find my own approach and musical sound".
