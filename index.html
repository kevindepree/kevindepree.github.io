Kevin Daniel DePree

Composer, Producer and Drummer

At 8 years old Kevin DePree started drumming on a kid sized drum set purchased from a flea market. DePreeâ€™s interest in drumming developed into a passion for music which has driven him to become the multifaceted artist he is today. His early career was a full time drummer in touring acts throughout the United States and Europe. He has gained the majority of his music knowledge on the band stand, performing styles from EDM to Jazz.

DePree got his start in television and film as an assistant for London based composer Sheridan Tongue. He was able to learn the art of film music, hands on, while contributing to several BBC 1 crime dramas as a studio musician. His training continued while co-writing alongside Tongue on several of his EMI Production Music albums as a programmer, sound designer, and composer. Naturally, DePree branched out on his own producing large amounts of works for various music libraries and indie films. His music has appeared on ABC, A&E, Discovery Channel, MTV, NFL, UFC, and numerous other Film and TV shows internationally.
